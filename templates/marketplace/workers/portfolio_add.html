Photo Tips
  • Use real photos of finished work — not stock images. Authenticity boosts your match score.
  • Good lighting matters. Well-lit photos give the AI more visual detail to encode.
  • Add a caption that describes the job context. This combines with the image for richer matching.
  • Upload at least 3–5 items. The more photos you have, the stronger your visual match profile.
  • Select the correct trade category — it's used to narrow which jobs your image is compared against.
How Image AI Works
Your photo is encoded by CLIP's visual encoder into a 512-dimension vector. When an employer posts a job with a description like "install solar panels on Lagos rooftop", that text is encoded into the same vector space — and your photo gets automatically matched by visual similarity.
